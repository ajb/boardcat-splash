BoardCat is an app for setting problems on your home climbing wall.

While other apps rely on a single image of your entire wall, BoardCat renders each hold individually. Why does this matter? With BoardCat, you can add or change holds without losing your existing problems. You can also do things like searching for problems by hold, or seeing which holds are unused in order to reset them.

BoardCat is currently in a closed beta. If you'd like to sign up to test the app, enter your email and we'll save you a spot in line.
